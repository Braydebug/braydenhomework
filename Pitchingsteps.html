Basic Steps for Pitching

Click on the images below:

Wind up step is small on in control.
Leg lift should also be in control and keep you on balance.
Make sure to break your hands with your leg as you bring it down.
Kick out your leg toward homeplate, making sure it stays in line.
Your front foot should land in line with home plate and your weight is on the balls of your feet.
Make sure to land heel to toe to get as close to the plate as possible.
Bring your glove into your chest for best accuracy.
Make sure you release the ball out front of your body for velocity.
